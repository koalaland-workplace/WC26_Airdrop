WC26 NFT Fantasy Rulebook
Nation Journey Airdrop - Overview
Nation Journey Airdrop is a two-stage program. Phase 1 (Viral Activation) is live and builds your nation rank, personal rank, tier progression and eligibility. Phase 2 (Tournament Ignition) is pre-announced and will execute conversion + distribution around World Cup 2026 snapshot timing.
Phase 1 - Viral Activation (Live) Phase 2 - Tournament Ignition (Pre-announced)
1. Token Framework & Pools
  • KICK is an off-chain participation point earned from mini games and tasks; it drives ranking, tier progression and allocation logic.
  • WC26 is the only on-chain token with fixed supply and non-inflationary rules defined in the Whitepaper.
1) National War Bonus Pool
1,000,000 WC26
Rewards top nations and qualified top contributors in Nation Wars.
2) Referral Champion Pool
1,000,000 WC26
Rewards Top 100 global referrers at the official World Cup 2026 snapshot.
3) Mini Games Conversion Pool
10,000,000 WC26
Dedicated pool to convert eligible KICK from approved mini games and tasks into WC26.
All allocations are fixed unless changed by an official governance decision.
2. How Your KICK Becomes WC26 (Future Conversion Logic)
2.1 Conversion Formula
WC26 per user = (Personal KICK ÷ Total System KICK) × 10,000,000 WC26
2.2 Eligibility Requirements
  • Accumulate at least 10,000 KICK.
  • Maintain at least 7 active days.
  • Successfully pass Anti-Sybil and compliance checks.
Failing any condition means KICK remains in-app but generates no WC26 in that conversion event.
2.3 Per-User Conversion Cap
  • Maximum counted KICK per user: 500,000 KICK.
  • KICK above 500k does not generate additional WC26.
  • This cap improves fairness and reduces extreme concentration.

2.5 Referral & Nation Bonus at Snapshot
  • Referral Champion Pool (1,000,000 WC26): Rank 1 x10; Rank 2-5 x5; Rank 6-10 x2.5; Rank 11-100 x1.
  • National War Bonus Pool (1,000,000 WC26): Top 5 nations receive 5 Legacy Boxes for Top 5 players, plus 1,000,000 WC26 for Top 50 players across those nations.
  • Top-50 personal multipliers: Top 1 x5; Top 2-5 x3; Top 6-10 x2; Top 11-50 x1.
3. How You Earn KICK (Viral Activation)
Phase 1 focuses on daily engagement, nation competition and social growth. Everything below is live gameplay.
3.1 Daily Lucky Spin
Access Rules
  • 1 free spin per day.
  • Invite 1 successfully registered user: +1 spin.
  • Share Nation War ranking: +1 spin.
  • Current app cap: 5 spins/day.
Reward Rates
  • 50 KICK - 30%
  • 100 KICK - 25%
  • 200 KICK - 10%
  • 2x Quiz today - 10%
  • 3x Referral today - 5%
  • Rising Box Ticket - 5%
  • Nothing - 15%
3.2 Penalty Challenge
  • Base match: two sides alternate penalties, five kicks per side.
  • When one side scores, that side gains +1 goal while opponent remains unchanged.
  • When one side misses, no score changes for either side.
  • Sudden death starts if tied after five kicks each.
  • In sudden death, both sides continue alternating one kick each.
  • First side to score while the opponent misses wins immediately (max five sudden-death rounds).
Solo Mode - Player vs AI
  • Play against a system-controlled goalkeeper or shooter.
  • 3 free matches per day.
  • Extra matches cost 500 KICK each.
  • Win: +2,000 KICK.
  • Loss: +0 KICK.
PvP Mode - Player vs Player
  • Real player vs real player, no AI influence.
  • No daily limit (subject to user balance).
  • Win: +2,000 KICK.
  • Loss: -2,500 KICK.
  • 500 KICK is permanently burned per match.
3.3 Nation Wars
WAR POINT = Total Nation KICK ÷ Number of Eligible Users
  • Eligible user: at least 5,000 KICK and active for 7+ days.
  • Nation lock: switch nation only after 7 days.
  • Weekly Top 5 nations: +5% KICK bonus (weekly earned KICK basis) + 5 Rising Boxes/nation for Top 5 players.
  • World Cup 2026 kickoff rulebook reward: Top 5 nations receive 5 Legacy Boxes for Top 5 players + 1,000,000 WC26 for Top 50 players across those nations.
3.4 Engage & Earn Tasks
  • Task reward cap per account: 15,000 KICK (tasks only, not game earnings).
  • All actions are subject to verification, Anti-Sybil screening and compliance review.
Main Task Categories (Examples)
  • Telegram (up to ~8,000 KICK): join global/national channels, join nation group and post 5 meaningful messages, join at least 1 official WAR vote.
  • Growth Actions (3,000 KICK): invite 3 active users (each active for at least 3 days), then invite 5 active users.
  • Amplification (1,000 KICK): share the WAR leaderboard and share a Pitch Pulse signal.
  • Twitter/X (3,000 KICK): follow official WC26, retweet selected post, like + meaningful comment, post original tweet with #WC26.
  • Facebook / Instagram (2,000 KICK): follow official page/profile, share official post, leave meaningful compliant comments, create original WC26 content.
  • YouTube (1,000 KICK): subscribe, watch at least 2 minutes (verified), comment on latest upload.
  • TikTok (1,000 KICK): follow official account, share official video, create original WC26-themed content.
In app display example: 0 of 28 tasks · CAP 15,000 KICK.
3.5 Referral Battle Royale
Instant Incentives
  • F1 registration: +200 KICK.
  • F1 active 7 days: +500 KICK.
  • F2 registration: +50 KICK.
  • F2 active 7 days: +100 KICK.
  • Season duration: 7 days per season.
  • Top 100 referrers: 10K-100K KICK + 5 Elite Boxes.
  • Rank 1 receives 1 extra Legacy Box.
  • Maximum 50 F1 counted per season; each F1 must reach ≥1,000 KICK.
  • Fraudulent, automated, or coordinated manipulation causes disqualification.
These rankings are also used later to split the 1,000,000 WC26 Referral Champion Pool at snapshot.
4. Compliance & Governance
  • Continuous anti-cheat and Anti-Sybil monitoring is enforced.
  • Allocation rules can be updated only via official governance announcements.
  • Snapshot timing for conversion and bonus pools will be publicly disclosed.
  • All rewards remain subject to validation and fair-play compliance review.
